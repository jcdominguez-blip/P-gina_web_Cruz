About Me

From a young age, I developed a strong affinity for design and art, which led me to study Graphic Design at the “Universidad de Buenos Aires” (FADU), specializing in corporate identities. As a freelance designer since 2018, I have worked on various projects, including identity brand for "Municipalidad de San Cristobal” and sub-brands for renowned companies like “Unión Ferroviaria” and “Trenes Argentinos”, where I focused on branding, communication, and application design.

My education and passion for design have allowed me to acquire skills in UX/UI, Community Management, and Front End Development. This portfolio reflects my commitment to creativity and the pursuit of effective solutions, inviting you to explore my projects and professional growth.
